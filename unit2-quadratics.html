Unit 2

Quadratic Equations

Quadratics appear everywhere — from the path of a thrown ball to the shape of satellite dishes. Learn to solve them fluently and you'll unlock a huge part of algebra.

What You'll Learn

  • What makes an equation "quadratic" and the standard form ax² + bx + c = 0
  • Factoring quadratics to find solutions quickly
  • Completing the square — a method that always works
  • The quadratic formula — the universal solver
  • Graphing parabolas: vertex, axis of symmetry, intercepts
