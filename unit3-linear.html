Unit 3

Linear Equations & Systems

Lines are the foundation of algebra. Master slope, intercepts, and systems of equations, and you'll have tools for solving real-world problems involving two unknowns.

What You'll Learn

  • Slope: what it means, how to calculate it, types of slope
  • Forms of a line: slope-intercept, point-slope, standard form
  • Graphing linear equations from their equation
  • Solving systems of equations by substitution
  • Solving systems of equations by elimination
