Unit 1

Functions

Functions are one of the most fundamental ideas in mathematics. Master them and the rest of algebra, calculus, and beyond becomes far more approachable.

What You'll Learn

  • What a function is, and what makes a relation not a function
  • How to evaluate functions by substituting values
  • Domain and range — the "legal" inputs and outputs of a function
  • Function notation and how to read f(x)
  • How to compose two functions together
